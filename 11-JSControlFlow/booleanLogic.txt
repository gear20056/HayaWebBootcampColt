Comparison Operators

> Greater than
>= Greater than or equal to

< Less than
<= Less than or equal to

== Equal to
!= Not Equal to

=== Equal value and type
!== Not Equal value or equal type


--------------------------------------
Assume x = 5

x > 10 [False]
x >= 5 [False]

x < -50 [False]
x <= 100 [True]

x == "5" [True]
x != "b" [True]

x === "5" [False]
x !== "5" [True]
